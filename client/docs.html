Cimexis.io Docs

General

Cimexis.io is a multiplayer shooter that supports the easy creation of bots to play competitively along side players. The documentation here will cover the basics of the game as well as how to put together a simple bot of your own.

Stats

Stats affect the strengths and weaknesses of a player. Each player is given 400 points which can be distributed in any way with up to 100 points allowed per stat.

  • health
    The maximum amount of health a player has.
  • regen
    How fast health regenerates over time.
  • speed
    The speed a player is able to move.
  • size
    The physical size of a player.
  • sight
    How far a player can see.
  • meleeDamage
    How much damage a player inflicts when touching another player. (cactus damage)
  • reload
    How fast a player can reload between shots.
  • damage
    How much damage bullets shot by the player will do. This does not affect meleeDamage.
  • bulletSpeed
    How fast a player's bullets move.
  • bulletSize
    The physical size of a player's bullets
  • knockback
    How hard other players will be knocked back when hit by the player's bullet.
  • kickback
    How much kickback the player will feel when shooting.
  • accuracy
    How accurate the player's shots will be to where they are aiming.

Weapon Classes

Before you start playing you will be given the chance to choose a single weapon. Each weapon has different strengths and weaknesses. Many require unique styles of play to be successful at.

Gun

For those that value simplicity

Sniper

For those who like to keep their distance

  • + x3 damage
  • + can see further
  • + can shoot further
  • - x4 longer to reload

Shot Gun

For those who don't like to aim

  • + burst of bullets
  • - inaccurate
  • - tiny range
  • - x2 longer to reload

Mini Gun

For those who hate reloading

  • + x3 faster reload
  • - inaccurate
  • - low range

Cannon

For those that don't want to ask twice

  • + x5 damage
  • + huge bullets
  • - slow bullets
  • - x5 longer to reload

Stealth

For those who are shy

  •    standing still

Charger

For those who like control

  • + build up charge when
  •    not shooting to make
  •    your next shot do more
  •    damage

Mine Layer

  • + lay mines
  • + x2 damage
  • - mines are stationary

Puncher

For those who want others to back off

  • + shoot fists

  • - inaccurate
  • - low range

Thruster

For Elon Musk

  • + x2 faster reload
  • + x3 kickback

  • - cannot move

For the indecisive

  • + double shot

  • - lower range

Spinner
